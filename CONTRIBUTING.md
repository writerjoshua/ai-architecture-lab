# Contributing to AI Architecture Lab

Thank you for your interest in this project. This is a personal portfolio/research project, but the patterns are designed to be studied and reproduced.

---

## What You Can Do

### Study and Reproduce

- Fork the repo
- Create your own instances of Sofia, Maya, or Diana
- Document your experience and variations
- Share insights (issues, discussions, or reach out directly)

### Report Issues

- Found a bug in the prompt files?
- Discovered an ambiguity in the documentation?
- Have suggestions for clarity?
- Open an issue or email connect@writerjoshua.com

---

## Guidelines

### For Conversations/Threads

If you're archiving conversations for publication:

1. Export the full conversation
2. Write a brief summary
3. Add a personal blog post reflecting on insights
4. Respect any sensitive context (no personal data, company secrets, etc.)

---

## Code of Conduct

- Be respectful and kind
- Assume good intent
- Credit others' contributions
- Keep discussions focused on the work
